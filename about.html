ABOUT
Jm Javillonar is a photographer/videographer/artist.
He was a Multimedia Arts student at the Lyceum of
the Philippines University at the time. His passion
was for the arts. Until he came into the realm of
photography. For him, photography was a vast concept.
To him, achieving perfection in each of his masterpieces
is essential in order to communicate the narrative
effectively. He founded 99 Visions to capture his many
visions such as events, portraiture, street, fashion,
landscape, and black and white. He believes that
portraying a message in each photo is crucial.
At his prime as a young man, he was only focused
in doing things that would help him grow more.
Failures are unavoidable, yet they serve as a learning
tool in the long run.
